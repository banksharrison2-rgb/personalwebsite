About

A senior at Tulane University's Freeman School of Business, studying Marketing and Management with a focus on how brands tell stories that actually land. My work has spanned experiential activations, influencer strategy, branded content, and consumer research — all oriented around one question: how do you make people genuinely connect with a product or idea?

Last summer, I joined the Bacardí Rum Brand Team in Miami, where I built an influencer-driven 360° marketing program targeting Gen Z in casual dining environments, helped bring the brand to life at Lollapalooza and other key activations, and collaborated across creative, insights, and partnerships teams on campaigns with real stakes.

I'm comfortable across tools like NielsenIQ, RStudio, Excel, and Canva, and equally at home in the content creation space — from Instagram strategy to short-form video. I move naturally between the analytical and the creative, and I hold myself to a high standard of quality in everything I put my name on.

Outside of work, I'm drawn to the things that quietly shape culture — finding a restaurant that shifts how you think about food, stumbling onto an album before anyone else does, watching a film that stays with you for days. I stay close to what's moving in music, fashion, food, and film, and I think that curiosity is a big part of what makes me good at this work.
